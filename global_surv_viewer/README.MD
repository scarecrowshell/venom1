## рҹҺҜ **Vision & Mission**

This project began as a simple 3D globe script with basic camera controls and rough location data. The goal is to evolve it into a **fullвҖ‘fledged, insanely detailed digital twin of Earth**вҖ”a "global thermal sonar live feed" that aggregates and visualizes virtually every free and public geospatial data source available.

We aim to democratize access to planetaryвҖ‘scale situational awareness by providing an open, extensible platform that rivals commercial offerings like Google Earth and Google Maps, but with deeper integration of realвҖ‘time environmental data, crowdsourced imagery, and scientific overlays.

## вҡҷпёҸ **Core Capabilities**

### рҹҢҗ **3D Earth Visualization Engine**

- **HighвҖ‘Performance Rendering:** Built on CesiumJS, an industryвҖ‘standard WebGL engine optimized for streaming massive geospatial datasets.
- **Global 3D Terrain:** Realistic elevation data sourced from NASA SRTM and ASTER GDEM, providing accurate mountain ranges, valleys, and bathymetry.
- **Smooth Navigation:** FreeвҖ‘form camera controls including pan, zoom, rotate, tilt, and flyвҖ‘to animations. Users can seamlessly transition from a global view down to street level.

### рҹҸў **Worldwide 3D Building Models**

- **OpenStreetMap Integration:** Over 350 million building footprints are extruded into 3D models using height and level data from OSM.
- **Dynamic Loading:** Buildings stream efficiently as 3D Tiles, ensuring smooth performance even in dense urban areas.
- **Future Enhancement:** Support for highвҖ‘resolution cityвҖ‘scale 3D models (e.g., Project PLATEAU, Microsoft Building Footprints).

### рҹӣ°пёҸ **MultiвҖ‘Source Satellite Imagery**

- **Daily Global Coverage:** TrueвҖ‘color imagery from NASA GIBS (MODIS Terra/Aqua, VIIRS) updated every 24 hours.
- **HighвҖ‘Resolution Mosaics:** SentinelвҖ‘2 cloudless composites (10m resolution) via public tile services.
- **Historical Basemaps:** Access to Landsat archives and other timeвҖ‘series imagery layers.
- **Custom WMS/WMTS Support:** Add any OGCвҖ‘compliant web map service as an overlay.

### рҹҡ¶ **StreetвҖ‘Level Immersion**

- **Mapillary Integration:** Seamlessly fetch and display geotagged streetвҖ‘view photos from the world's largest crowdsourced imagery platform.
- **ClickвҖ‘toвҖ‘Explore:** Users can click any road or location to view the nearest groundвҖ‘level 360В° photo.
- **KartaView Compatibility:** Optional integration with KartaView (formerly OpenStreetCam) for additional coverage.

### рҹҢЎпёҸ **RealвҖ‘Time Environmental Overlays**

- **Thermal Infrared "Live Feed":** NearвҖ‘realвҖ‘time infrared imagery from geostationary weather satellites (e.g., Meteosat, GOES) via the Geosatellite API, updated every 10вҖ“60 minutes.
- **Active Fire Detection:** NASA FIRMS and MODIS thermal anomaly layers showing current wildfire hotspots.
- **Flood & Disaster Monitoring:** Overlays from NASA GIBS and Copernicus Emergency Management Service for floods, storms, and other events.
- **Weather Radar & Precipitation:** Animated weather radar and precipitation maps (future integration).

### рҹ”Қ **Global Search & Geocoding**

- **Nominatim Geocoder:** Free, OpenStreetMapвҖ‘based global place search supporting addresses, landmarks, and coordinates.
- **Reverse Geocoding:** Click anywhere on the globe to retrieve the nearest address or point of interest.
- **Smart Suggestions:** Autocomplete and fuzzy matching for rapid location discovery.

### рҹ§© **Extensible Data Layer System**

- **Modular Overlay Architecture:** Add, remove, and reorder imagery and data layers at runtime.
- **Vector Data Support:** Display GeoJSON, KML, and CZML data for custom annotations, boundaries, and dynamic objects.
- **3D Tiles Streaming:** Efficiently stream massive point clouds, photogrammetry meshes, and BIM models.

### рҹҺӣпёҸ **Advanced UI & Controls**

- **Layer Manager Panel:** Toggle visibility, adjust opacity, and reorder all active data layers.
- **TimeвҖ‘Dynamic Playback:** (In development) Scrub through historical satellite imagery and sensor data.
- **Measurement Tools:** Calculate distances, areas, and volumes directly on the 3D globe.
- **Customizable Basemap:** Switch between satellite, terrain, street map, and custom tile providers.

### рҹ”җ **Backend & Data Aggregation (Optional)**

- **API Proxy Service:** A lightweight Node.js or Python backend handles API key management, request aggregation, caching, and rateвҖ‘limit compliance for thirdвҖ‘party services.
- **Data Fusion Pipeline:** PreвҖ‘process and combine disparate data formats into CesiumвҖ‘compatible 3D Tiles or imagery layers.
- **Offline Caching:** Support for local storage of tiles and data for disconnected operation.
